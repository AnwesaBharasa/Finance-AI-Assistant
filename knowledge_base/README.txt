# 📚 Permanent Knowledge Base

This folder is the "Brain" of your Finance AI Assistant. 

### How to use:
1.  **Drop PDFs here**: Copy any financial documents, tax guides, or investment policies (PDF format) into this folder.
2.  **Sync within the App**: Go to the Finance AI Chatbot sidebar and click the **"🔄 Refresh Library"** button.
3.  **Chat**: Once indexed, the AI will use these documents as its primary source of truth in **Document Search** mode.

### Example Documents to Add:
- Income Tax Slab Guides
- RBI Annual Reports
- Company Annual Reports (for stock analysis)
- Personal Insurance Policy details
